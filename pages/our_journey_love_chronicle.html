When Destiny Made Us Meet

July 07, 2014

Pavithra & Karthik's Wedding, Chennai

"Sometimes the heart sees what is invisible to the eye"

On that day, during the very first roll call, destiny brought you into my life in the most unexpected way. I was late and stood outside the classroom, and as you rose to answer your name, my eyes met yours. In that single glance, something unexplainable awakened in me. Time stood still, the world faded, and all that remained was the silent connection between us. I never believed in love at first sight, but in that moment, I knew my heart had found its home. Your eyes held a promise, a warmth that touched my soul, and from that day forward, my life has never been the same. Today, as I stand before you, I vow to cherish that first glance forever, to honor the love it created, and to treasure you for all the days of my life.

The Day Our Hearts Smiled

March 8, 2020

Cafe Mocha, T. Nagar

"Coffee tastes better when shared with someone special"

What was meant to be just a quick coffee in the college canteen soon turned into something unforgettable. That simple meeting blossomed into long, effortless conversations where we spoke about everything—our dreams, our families, our favorite books, and even our deepest fears. With every word, I felt myself drawn closer, as if our souls already knew each other. Aravind made me laugh until my cheeks ached, and in that laughter, I found a comfort I had never known before. In that moment, I realized this wasn’t just a conversation—it was the beginning of a love story, one that I wanted to keep writing with him for the rest of my life.

The Perfect Proposal

December 24, 2023

Marina Beach, Chennai

"Will you be my forever?"

I still remember that college function when she walked in wearing a saree. The moment I saw you, I was completely stunned—my words failed me, and my heart forgot how to beat. In that traditional attire, you looked so graceful, so radiant, like an angel who had stepped into my world. From that day, I realized that my admiration for you was only growing deeper and stronger. Not long after, I gathered my courage and asked you the question that had been living in my heart: “Would you like to marry me?” You smiled and said you needed some time to answer. And now, as I stand here before you, surrounded by everyone we love, we both know the answer. Today, I vow to never stop seeing you with the same awe, to always treasure your grace, and to love you endlessly—just as I did in that unforgettable moment, and for every moment yet to come.
